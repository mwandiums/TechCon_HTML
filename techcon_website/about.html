About TechCon 2024

History

Founded in 2010, TechCon began as a small gathering of tech enthusiasts focused on emerging technologies like cloud computing and big data. The event quickly grew, expanding into a multi-day conference by 2013, with a broader range of topics such as artificial intelligence, blockchain, and cybersecurity.

By 2016, TechCon had outgrown its original venue, attracting thousands of attendees from around the world. The conference featured interactive workshops, startup pitch competitions, and hackathons, solidifying its status as a global tech event.

In 2020, TechCon embraced virtual participation, reaching an even larger, global audience, and covering cutting-edge topics like quantum computing, 5G, and the Metaverse.

Today, in 2024, TechCon is one of the world's largest tech conferences, with over 10,000 attendees and a focus on inspiring, educating, and connecting the global tech community. With an international reach and a diverse range of topics, TechCon continues to drive innovation and shape the future of technology.

This condensed version highlights the key milestones and growth of TechCon, focusing on its evolution from a small event to a global tech conference.

Mission

To foster innovation, collaboration, and the exchange of knowledge by bringing together technology professionals, industry leaders, and enthusiasts to explore cutting-edge advancements and drive the future of technology.
